The 9-Day Expedition Plan

1

Flight to Lima

Meet your Tour Director at the airport.

2

Explore Lima

Colonial heart, San Francisco Monastery catacombs, and seaside Miraflores stroll.

3

Lima - Cuzco

Fly to Cuzco. Explore hillside ruins and the fortress of Sacsayhuamán.

4

Sacred Valley

Pisac Market, wildlife sanctuary, and Ollantaytambo Fortress.

5

Sacred Valley - Machu Picchu

Scenic train journey. Guided exploration of the Lost City of the Incas.

6

Citadel Exploration

Self-guided time at Machu Picchu followed by a train back toward Cuzco.

7

Rafting & Farewell

Urubamba River rafting and a special farewell dinner.

8

Free Time - Home

Final time in Cuzco before flying to Lima and departing for home.

9

Arrival Home

Welcome home!
